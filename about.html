Lucknow University

Founded in 1867

University of Lucknow is public authorised university founded in 1867 with a motto of Light and Learning located in capital city of Uttar Pradesh. Lucknow University established in 1920 with a wide campus area of 220 acres. With over more than 160 affiliated collages and 1,00,000 +students, the university offers undergraduate, postgraduate and research courses in various streams such as Arts, Science, Commerce, Law, and FineArts.

Lucknow University

The Faculty of Law, University of Lucknow is a pioneer legal institution established in 1921. The real architect was Prof. R. U. Singh with keenness and enthusiasm organized teaching and research in the faculty in the systematic way. The Faculty has produced many legal personalities including former President of India, Supreme Court and High Court Judges, law makers and etc. The Faculty include experts’ legal members and bright students with brilliant minds. Legal Aid Clinic

Legal Aid Clinic is an initiative of the Faculty of Law, University of Lucknow with the determination to promote 'EQUAL ACCESS TO JUSTICE’. The centre is a specialized organ of the faculty of law which is playing active role in providing legal aid to the public at large. It intends to provide the free legal aid to those unprivileged, marginalised or deprived classes of society. The Centre was established with the objective to promote legal awareness by means of pro-bono programs like Consultation Camps, Workshops, Seminars, Menstrual Hygiene and Cleanliness Drives, Legal Aid and Awareness Sessions and Legal Literacy Programs. The Centre invites various practicing advocates and legal academicians to render their support to the indigent. The Legal Aid Clinic has collaboration with District Legal Services Authority of Lucknow, other social institutions and other NGOs of Lucknow. The Centre has three paralegal volunteers deputed by DLSA. The services of our centre is accessible to all seven days of week with the timing of 2:00 pm to 6:00 pm.
